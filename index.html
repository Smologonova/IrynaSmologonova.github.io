About me

I am a data analyst with a background in product development, financial auditing and customer service. With my curiosity and tenacity, I make connections between different data sets, translate data into actionable insights and communicate the ideas to stakeholders.

My recent work as a staff accountant and retail store manager leveraged my ability in testing and drawing conclusions to make improvements in monitoring KPIs, customer recruitment and retention as well as communication across multiple industries.
